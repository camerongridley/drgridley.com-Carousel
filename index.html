Offering Couples Therapy, Sex Therapy and Individual Therapy. For All of Denver.

As a licensed clinical psychologist and certified sex therapist in Denver, I specialize in couples therapy and sex therapy. I also provide individual counseling for a wide range of issues, including anxiety, depression and dealing with trauma, among others.

If you would like to schedule an appointment or just ask questions, please call me at (303) 720-6177 or email me at camerongridley@gmail.com.

Working together for a solution. You're not alone.

My collaborative style of therapy focuses on your individual needs. It centers on us working together to uncover the best solutions for you, helping resolve relationship problems and achieve personal goals.

Everyone deserves happiness. Period.

I have experience with – and an affinity for – working with culturally diverse groups, including members of the gay and lesbian community.
